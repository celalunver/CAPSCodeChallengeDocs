StackEditPro can sync one file with multiple locations and accounts.</p><h2 id="synchronize-a-file">Synchronize a file</h2><br /><p>Once your file is linked to a synchronized location, StackEditPro will periodically synchronize it by downloading/uploading any modification. This allows you to list and remove synchronized locations that are linked to your file.</p><h1 id="publication">Publication</h1><br /><p>Publishing in StackEditPro makes it simple for you to publish online your files.
